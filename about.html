About Us

Employees are the pillar behind a company's success

The main objective of this Labour Management System project is to have a completely automated project allotment system which can even be managed by a non technical person. This project will serve the purpose of maintaining all the contractor and sub-contractor details along with issuing registration certificate to contractor and license certificate to sub-contractor. This Labour Management System project will definitely reduce the time and effort in searching for contractors with expired licenses and intimating them beforehand. Fetching all the contractor details, sub-contractor details and work order details will be made easy with just a single mouse click. Overall management of Contract labor will be easy and quick. Today, labours look for an labour-friendly environment where their ideas thrive, while the traditional work culture forces most-able labours to resign and look for another job due to low wages of the workers, more than 12 hours of working time etc.

A labour management system provides labour productivity reporting and planning capabilities. The planning capabilities provide the ability to analyse workforce requirements given a certain amount of work to be performed and a standard unit of time to perform each element of work. Labor productivity planning capabilities provide the ability to measure and report the performance of individuals, groups or facilities vs. a predefined standard for performing each defined element of work. The software records the optimal time to carry out each task and compares it with actual performance. The software also stores the various information of the labours such their working experience, daily incomes, jobs done in past etc. The app can also record the attendance of the labours

The idea is completely based on Labour Management Information System in which the data is synchronised and stored which can be used further as a record of the jobs done in past by the labours. Also this app will make the labours learn new techniques by using videos that are preloaded in the app. This app will help the labours as well as the employees as they can monitor the working hours of labours, tasks completed, assign different tasks to different labours, Daily payment records of labours etc.

Ideas and market opportunities that we plan to address in the application

We develop some ideas and market opportunities that can be considered addressing in the labour market management application. They are described below

1. Skill Maching and Job Recommendation:-We create and develop an AI-powered system that matches job seeker’s skills, qualification and preferences withavailable job openings. Then it will provide personalized job recommendations to help job seekers findthe most suitable opportunities quickly.

2. Remote Work Management:-We have to develop features to support the growing trend to remote work , including remote job listings , virtual collaboration tools and performance tracking for remote employees

3. Upskilling and Training Program:-Here we develop an option or incorporate a learning component that suggests relevant training courses or certifications to help job seekers improve their skills and increase their employability.

4. Resume/ CV Optimisatioin:-Here we’ll offer tools to help job seekers to create effective resumes or CV’s, with the help of which they can get resumes and CVs easily and which provide real time feedback to improve their chances of getting noticed by the employers.

5. Lifestyle and Location Preferences:-Here we develop a feature so that Job seekers can filter job opportunities based on factors like commute distance , flexible work hours and preferred work locations.

6. Feedback and Rating System:-Here we develop a system where both employers and employees can provide feedback and ratings after each job placement, which helps to build a reputation and credibility for job seekers and employers alike.

Systematic Plan to Put out the Idea

Taking the Labor Information Management System (LIMS) idea to the markets requires a well-thought-out plan. Here's a detailed strategy to achieve this:

Market Research:-We Conduct extensive market research to identify potential target industries, competitors, and the specific labor information management needs of businesses. Understand the pain points and challenges faced by employers and workers related to labor information.

2. Concept Refinement:-Clearly define the objectives and scope of the LIMS. Identify the key features and functionalities that will address the pain points of employers and workers related to labor information management.

3. Market Validation:-Conduct thorough market research to validate the demand for the LIMS. Engage with potential users, businesses, and industry experts to gather feedback and assess the market's readiness for such a solution.

4. Value Proposition:-Clearly define the unique value proposition of the LIMS. Highlight how it can streamline labor information processes, improve efficiency, reduce compliance risks, and enhance decision-making for businesses.

5. Prototype Development:-Build a functional prototype of the LIMS to demonstrate its core features and functionalities. Use the prototype to gather feedback

6. Regulatory Compliance:-Ensure the LIMS complies with relevant labor laws, data privacy regulations, and industry standards. Emphasize data security and privacy measures to build trust with potential customers.

7. Targeted Marketing:-Develop a targeted marketing strategy to reach the intended audience. Utilize online channels, social media platforms, industry events, and partnerships to create awareness about the LIMS.

8. Pricing Model:-Determine a competitive pricing model that aligns with the value provided by the LIMS. Consider offering different tiers or subscription plans to cater to the needs and budgets of various businesses.

9. Beta Testing:-Launch a beta version of the LIMS and invite select businesses to participate in testing. Gather feedback, address any issues, and fine-tune the system before the official release.

10. Launch Campaign:-Plan a well-coordinated launch campaign to generate excitement around the LIMS. Use promotional activities, discounts, and limited-time offers to encourage early adoption.

11. Customer Support:-Establish a dedicated customer support team to assist users during onboarding and throughout their usage of the LIMS. Offer resources like tutorials and documentation to help them maximize the system's benefits.

12. Customer Success Stories:-Share success stories and case studies of businesses that have benefited from using the LIMS. Use these testimonials to build trust and credibility in the market.
